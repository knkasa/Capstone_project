Logistic regression

The results for the logistic linear regression is shown. Here, the sigmoid function is used to fit the data. As shown in histograms, not all independent variables play important roles. Some of those variables are redundent and can be excluded from the model. Here, we determined such variables using Elimination method and looking at its p-values. We throw out variables that have p-value greater than the threshold ~0.2. The table shown below shows the variables that are included in the model.

Variable name Coefficient
Credit score -0.08
Age 1.08
Balance 0.21
Is active member -0.65
Live in Germany 0.49
Male -0.34

After normalizing each variables, we may determine which varibles play an important role by looking the coefficients (coefficients that appear in the fitting function). Here, the positive coefficients mean these variables make customers leave the bank. One can see "Age" plays the most important role. Another word, older generation tend to leave the bank. while "Active Member" and "Geography" also play some role in making customers leave.
