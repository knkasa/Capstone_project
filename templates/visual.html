Visualization

The above figures are taken from each independent variables in the dataset. Colors correspond to customers stayed/left the bank as indicated in the legend. The numbers above each bars correspond to the percentage of those who stayed/left the bank for each bins.

Comparing percentages across different bins, one can see which variables play an important role in making customers leave. For example, "Age" and "Geography" seems to play a role while "Tenure" and "Has Credit Card" seem to play less role.
